PERIYAR NATIONAL PARK

Periyar National Park and Wildlife Sanctuary were established in the year 1982 and has been attracting a number of tourists since then. Located near the districts of Idukki, Pathanamthitta, and Kottayam, this national park is constituted of a tiger reserve as well as an elephant reserve. Spread over an area of almost 925-kilometer squares, Periyar National Park park is composed of some of the rarest, endangered, and endemic flora and fauna and also watersheds of two crucial rivers which include Periyar and Pamba.The distinctive beauty of the national park and the collection of exotic species is what makes the attraction one in a million. People from all over the country and also visitors from outside the country come to visit this place to enjoy the panoramic beauty of the national park. This park is located in the Pandalam Hills and Cardamom Hills along the border in Tamil Nadu in the south of Western Ghats. The location itself is quite remarkable which makes its beauty one of a kind. If you are planning a trip to the South, then visiting this national park is a must.
