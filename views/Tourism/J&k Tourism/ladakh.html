Ladakh is a Himalayan region administered by India as a union territory. Ladakh is the largest and the second least populous union territory of India. Ladakh is the highest plateau in India with much of it being over 3,000 m (9,800 ft). It extends from the Himalayan to the Kunlun Ranges and includes the upper Indus River valley.
A bike ride once in a Lifetime to Ladakh is the Dream of almost all of us.
