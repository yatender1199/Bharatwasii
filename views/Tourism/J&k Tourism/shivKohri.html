Shiv Kohri

The Lord Shiva's Cave
< Go Back

Shiv Khori means lord Shiva's cave, is a place dedicated to lord Shiva centuries ago. This place is frequented by the devotees of lord Shiva to worship the lingam inside the cave. The cave is around 200m long and around 200 people can stand in the entrance itself.
About 40 to 50-year ago, only a few people knew about the Shiv Khori shrine but it has gained much popularity during decades.
