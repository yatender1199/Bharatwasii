Mandarmani The Developing One

The seaside village of Mandarmani in East Medinipur locale is situated at the northern end of the Bay of Bengal. It is one of the biggest and quick developing resort villages of West Bengal and is located about 180 km from Kolkata. It is contended to be the longest drive-in seashore in India. One can relax, swim and appreciate each moment of their stay at this majestic seashore. The minute one gets worn out, they can chill off with green coconut water. There are very few visitors around and that is the best part of this seashore. One can spend their time sunbathing and watching the birds and waves.It is Located about 3 km away from Shankarpur, the beach of Mandar mani is completely deserted.
