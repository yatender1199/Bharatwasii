MASHOBRA

Located in the lap of Himalayas,Mashobra is a quaint little hill retreat in Shimla district of Himanchal Pradesh.Lush oak forests,beautiful fruit orchards and fast-flowing streams make it a mesmerizing tourism destination. Mashobra is a delight for nature lovers,adventure seekers,spiritual travellers and casual holiday seekers.
