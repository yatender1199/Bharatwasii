KINNAUR

Nestled in the northeast corner of Himanchal Pradesh bordering Tibet to the east,Kinnaur has three high mountains ranges including the magnificant Zanskar,Dhauladhar and Himalayas that encloses enchanted valleys of Sutlej,Spiti,Baspa and their tributaries.

The slopes are loaded with thick wood,well developed orchards of apples and apricots,fields and hamlets which makes it an extraordinary tourism spot and remains snow covered for six months.The most important destination in this region is religious natural rock 'Shiva Lingam',that is nestled at the peak of Kinnaur Kailash Mountain.It has an influence of Tibetian culture because it is at Indo-tibetan border

Kinnaur is divided into three administrative areas-Pooh,Kalpa and Nichar.Kalpa is one of the most fascinating village in the region.Kinnaur is a great spot for adventure sports like trekking and skiing,and camping.
