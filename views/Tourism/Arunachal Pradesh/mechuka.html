Mechuka

Mechuka is a small town nestled 6,000 feet above sea level in the Mechuka Valley in Shi-Yomi District of Arunachal Pradesh, India. Name of current MLA of Mechuka constituency is Pasang Dorjee Sona. After Menchuka the McMahon border separates Indian territory and Chinese territory.Menchukha is situated in a forested valley, surrounded by pine trees and thornbushes. The river Yargyapchu flows in the valley of Menchukha.
